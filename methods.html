Approaches and Methods that I use:

Adaptation to level

We will start by memorizing the most meaningful words and subjects according to your level and needs.

Learning with context

We learn words and grammar with an example in a sentence to better understand their meaning. The more a word makes sense to you, the faster you will memorize it.

Concentration on Speaking

Learning a language includes learning how to recognize and reproduce its sounds. This is actually how everybody has ever learned their own language: through sounds. So, we will focus, at first, on the oral aspect of the language.

Change of activity

We don't stay the whole lesson with one boring textbook. I select different resources and various approaches according to the different needs of my students.

Listen to the language

Nowadays, there are plenty of methods to do this: you can watch TV, films or series, listen to music, radio, etc. I recommend my students to use it all.
